What this is

Senior-care dietitians review weekly menus against every resident's diet order, allergen profile, and texture modification — often in spreadsheets, often manually. ds-meal pairs a deterministic API with two focused agentic workflows (AI menu planner + natural-language ordering) to move the reasoning out of the spreadsheet and into the tool.

Phase 1 demonstrates the foundation. Phase 2 (Inngest, richer wiki compile, multi-tenant, supplier ERP) is documented alongside every Phase 1 seam.
